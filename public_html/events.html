Battle Of The Dens

Battle of the dens is the summer coronation. Hundreds of Amtguarders will come from far and wide to honor the crowing of the northern monarchy. The event is held in August each year and will require you to bring apropriate clothing and a tent. Talk with us on facebook if you are new, the event is not held at our typical feilding location due to its size.

Frostbite

Frostbite is the winter corination for the dutchy of wolvenfang. In the darkest of days true heros feast! The event is held each year in Feburary. It is located at confederation high school, come join us for tournements and role play!
